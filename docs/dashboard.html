Decision Pipeline
1. Rate Collection
Fetches live APY from Aave V3, Morpho Blue, Compound V3 on Base. Cross-validates on-chain reads + DeFi Llama.
2. Risk Scoring
5 factors: TVL (25%), age (20%), audits (20%), utilization (20%), bad debt (15%). Higher = riskier.
3. Net APY
Subtracts round-trip gas (approve + supply + withdraw) over 90 days. Rejects net APY ≤ 0.
4. Allocation
risk_adjusted_yield = net_apy * (1 - risk_score). Proportional allocation, 40% cap per protocol, 10% reserve.
